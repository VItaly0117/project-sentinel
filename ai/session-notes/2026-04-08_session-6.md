## Open risks
- The walkthrough still depends on the operator supplying correctly shaped local raw files.
- There is still no downloader/paginator for historical backfills.
- The inspect helper validates one normalized dataset at a time; it is not a catalog or experiment tracker.
- Training still uses OHLC-based assumptions and ignores microstructure effects.
